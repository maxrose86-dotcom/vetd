FAQ

Common questions

For contributors
How much can I earn?
+
Studies pay £10–£80 depending on length and format. Short online surveys typically pay £10–£20. 60–90 minute video sessions pay £40–£80. Elite contributors can access studies paying up to £100 per session.
What kinds of studies are there?
+
Online surveys, video call interviews, in-person sessions, diary studies, and product testing at home. You can set your preferred formats in your profile and we'll only show you what fits your preferences.
How does the rating system work?
+
After each study, the researcher rates you on 5 dimensions: punctuality, engagement quality, honesty, thoughtfulness of responses, and how well your actual profile matched their criteria. Your overall score is a weighted average across all your completed studies.
How and when do I get paid?
+
Payment is processed automatically when the researcher marks the study as complete and confirms your participation. Standard processing takes up to 14 days. Elite contributors receive priority payment processing.
For companies
How quickly can I get participants?
+
Most studies receive their first applicants within a few hours of going live. For standard studies, you can typically have all sessions booked within 24–48 hours. Complex targeting criteria may take slightly longer.
How are contributors screened?
+
Contributors complete a detailed profile covering demographics, interests, employment, and household situation. Every contributor has a verified email address. BankID identity verification is coming soon. Contributor ratings are visible to you before you confirm any applicant.
Can I filter participants by demographics?
+
Yes. When posting a study you can target by age range, gender, location, employment status, interests, household income, and living situation. You can also set a minimum rating requirement to filter for only highly-rated contributors.
What's the platform fee exactly?
+
You set the participant pay rate. We add a flat 20% platform fee on top of that. For example: if you pay participants £50 each, the total cost per participant to you is £60. There are no listing fees, no monthly subscriptions, and no minimum spend requirements.
